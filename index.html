PHOTOSCANSUMMARY

Got a lot of images you want to read from?

Try PhotoScanSummary

PhotoScanSummary extract the text within an image, with support for broad range of languages,
along with language identification and then provide its summary in an efficient manner
for ease of understanding, that is done mainly by reducing text to its most important sentences.

Try it!

Features


Ease of Use

Extremely easy to use as you just need to upload your image with content in it and you get its summarized version within minutes.

Time Saving

Instead of studying the big image content for hours and then summarizing it, let the application do it for you within a minute .

Precise

Gives you a concise and to the point summary with only the most important lines depending on the number of lines wanted.

User Guidelines

Getting summary from your image is so easy!
Just follow the instructions given below and you will get it in no time




  •      Step 1: Choose an image that has text data in it so that it won't return empty data.

  •     Step 2: Upload an image using the "Choose File" or the "Drag-And-Drop" option given.

  •     Step 3: Click "Submit" to send the data to the server.

  •     Step 4: You can now see the summarized data in the textbox.
